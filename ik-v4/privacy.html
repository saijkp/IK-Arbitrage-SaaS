Official Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 23, 2026

At IK ENG, we take your data privacy seriously. This policy outlines how the IK Arbitrage Terminal Chrome Extension handles information to provide you with expert sourcing and logistics data.

1. Data We Collect

  • User-Provided Information: We collect your email address only if you voluntarily sign up for our waitlist or newsletter. We receive your phone number and message content only if you click the "Secure This Deal" button to contact our logistics team via WhatsApp.
  • Browsing Data: The extension only activates on Amazon product pages (amazon.co.uk and amazon.com). We do not track, store, or monitor your browsing history on any other websites.

2. How We Use Data

We use your information strictly to provide logistics quotes, sourcing verification, and updates regarding the IK Arbitrage Terminal. We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for marketing purposes.

3. Data Security

All data transmitted between the extension and our servers is handled via secure HTTPS connections. We use industry-standard encryption and security protocols to protect your information from unauthorized access.

4. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data. You may request the deletion of your information or opt-out of our communications at any time by contacting us at: info@ikeng.co.uk.
